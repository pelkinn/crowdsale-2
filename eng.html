Toolkits

The Crowdsale Network project is a self-regulatory system with its own infrastructure, social and economic mechanics, implemented with the help of multifunctional tools that will be available for each user.
VQ Education
It is a personal educational trainer which contains base of knowledge in the trends directions like blockchain technology.
CSN Pay
A multi-currency crypto wallet with a high level of reliability and quick installation on a computer and with a guarantee of safe storage. It supports all popular crypto-currencies and tokens of all start-ups created on the CSN platform.
CSN Store
It is an Internet site operating in the field of E-commerce, which carries out B2B and B2C trading transactions where the trading unit of the calculation is CSN/BTC and other topical cryptocurrencies.
CSN Exchange
It is an exchange which sells the trade of current cryptocurrencies and tokens of successful projects of the Crowdsale Network platform.
CSN Charity
Association of charitable foundations on the basis of technology of block-houses, which includes its own charitable foundation platform "I change the world"
CSN Media
A tool for implementing the news flow in the form of online broadcasting, news broadcasts.
CSN Travel
On-site seminars, international conferences, travel, intensive trainings through the Crowdsale Network platform for effective representatives, franchisees.
